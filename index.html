Our Story

A Café Born from Passion

Brewed Haven opened in 2012 on a quiet lane in Old Faridabad with one vision — to create a space where quality coffee and warm hospitality truly meet.

Every bean is sourced ethically, every dish prepared with care. We believe a great cup of coffee can change your morning, and a welcoming space can change your day.
